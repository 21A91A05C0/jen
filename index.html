Gudisa

DEPICTION OF GUDISA

Gudisa is the only grassland tourist destination in Andhra Pradesh, 45-km away from Maredumilli town in East Godavari Agency. Located in the reserve forest, the tiny hamlet of Gudisa is surrounded by fields of flowers (Lamiaceae family), which bloom in winter in hundreds of acres on the Gudisa hilltop, and a waterfall, wooing tourists from across Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. This winter, Gudisa hill has been witnessing a huge rush of tourists and tourism activities. We are arranging Vehicles, Camps, Food and Campfire to make your trip more beautiful.

Located in the picturesque hills of AP, Gudisa is one of the less explored forests in South India. The region is famous for its thick jungles and the Maredumilli park, which is the perfect way to experience the natural beauty of this hill station.
